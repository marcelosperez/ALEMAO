            MALE
||||||||||||||||||||||||||||||||||||
||||||||||||||||||||||||||||||||||||



ANIMALS, CARS, CURRENCY (MALE)
Common exceptions: die Mark (former German currency), das Pfund (British pound), die Krone (Danish currency)
DAYS, MONTHS, SEASONS (MALE)
Common exceptions: das Jahr (year), die Nacht (night), die Woche (week)
DIRECTIONS (MALE)

DRINKS (alcoholic, plant-based) (MALE)
Common exception: das Bier (beer)
MOUNTAINS, 
MOUNTAIN RANGES (MALE)
Common exceptions: die Eifel, die Haardt, die Rhön, die Sierra Nevada, and mountains / mountain ranges that are actually compound nouns (compound nouns take the gender of the final noun): das Erzgebirge, das Matterhorn, die Zugspitze
ROCKS, MINERALS (MALE)
Common exceptions: das Erz (ore), die Kohle (coal), die Kreide (chalk), das Mineral (mineral)
NON-GERMAN RIVERS (MALE)
Common exceptions: non-German rivers ending in -a or -e, e.g.: die Seine, die Themse (Thames), die Wolga
OUTER SPACE (MALE)
Common Exceptions: die Sonne (sun), die Venus, die Erde (Earth)
 
WEATHER (MALE)
Common exceptions: die Sonne (sun), die Wolke (cloud), die Brise (breeze), das Eis (ice), das Gewitter (thunderstorm), die Graupel (hail), das Wetter (weather), die Witterung (atmospheric conditions)
NOUNS FROM STRONG VERBS (MALE)
der Betrieb (operation, company, plant), der Biss (bite, from bissen), der Fall (case, instance; drop, from fallen), der Gang (gear; aisle)
Common exceptions: das Grab (grave), das Leid (harm, sorrow), das Maß (measurement), das Schloss (castle), das Verbot (ban, prohibition)


-ANT -AST -ICH 
-IG -LING -OR -US
-NER -ISMUS -EICH (MALE)
Examples: der Konsonant (konsonant), der Kontrast (contrast), der Teppich (rug, carpet), der Käfig (cage), der Schwächling (weakling), der Faktor (factor), der Zirkus (circus)

-EN -EL -ER (60% NOUNS) (MALE)
der Hafen (harbor), der Flügel (wing), der Schatten (shade), der Fehler (mistake) and all nouns referring to people (most are from verbs), e.g. der Bäcker (baker, from backen), der Fahrer (driver, from fahren), but also der Onkel (uncle), der Vater (father), etc.
Some common exceptions all gerunds such as das Essen (the food, eating) or das Fahren (driving); die Butter (butter), die Regel (rule), die Wurzel (root), die Geisel (hostage), das Fieber (fever), das Segel (sail), das Zeichen (sign, symbol)
NOTE: there are no -en nouns that are feminine!














             NEUTER
||||||||||||||||||||||||||||||||||||
||||||||||||||||||||||||||||||||||||



ALPHABET (NEUTER)
das “A”, das “B”, das “Fis” (F-sharp), das “Des” (D-flat)

CONTINENTS, CITIES,
PROVINCES (NEUTER)
das Afrika, das London, das Frankreich (France), das Bayern (Bavaria) Common exceptions: die Arktis (Artic), die Antarktis (Antarctica), die Schweiz (Switzerland) and other provinces or countries, including those that end in -a, -e, -ei, and -ie (e.g. die Türkei) that are gendered feminine with the exception of Afrika and China, which are still neuter; some countries are masculine: der Iran, der Jemen, der Kongo, der Libanon, der Sudan.
GERUNDS, COLORS, LANGUAGES (NEUTER)
das Singen (singing), das Blau (blue), das Spanisch, das Meeting, das Nein (no)

HOTELS, CAFES, RESTAURANTS, MOVIE THEATERS (NEUTER)
das Marriott, das “Hard-Rock”, das Hilton, das “Grand Rex”

METALS, CHEMICAL ELEMENTS (NEUTER)
das Gold, das Kobalt (cobalt), das Blei (lead), das Eisen (iron), das Kupfer (copper) Common exceptions: die Bronze (bronze), der Phospor (phosphorus), der Schwefel (sulfur), der Stahl (steel), also metals or chemical elements that are compounds such as der Sauerstoff (oxygen)
SCIENTIFIC UNITS (NEUTER)
das Atom, das Elektron, das Volt, das Watt Common exceptions: Liter and Meter having varying gender. (Read more in the Noun Outliers section.)
YOUNG PERSONS /
BABY ANIMALS (NEUTER)
das Baby, das Kind (child), das Ferkel (piglet), das Fohlen (foal), das Lamm (lamb)


-CHEN -LEIN -ICHT 
-IL -IT -MA -MENT -TEL
-TUM -UM (NEUTER)
das Mädchen (girl), das Fräulein (young woman, miss), das Dickicht (thicket), das Ventil (valve), das Dynamit (dynamite), das Schema (schematic), das Experiment, das Viertel (quarter), das Christentum (christianity), das Museum, das Individuum (individual)
Common exceptions: der Profit, der Granit, die Firma (company), der Zement (cement), der Reichtum (wealth), der Irrtum (error)

GE- (MOST NOUNS STARTING WITH GE) (NEUTER)
das Gesetz (law), das Gespräch (conversation), das Gebäude (building)
Common exceptions: der Gedanke (thought), der Geschmack (taste), and approx. 20 other masculine and feminine nouns that start with Ge- not counting any Ge- nouns referring to male or female persons (e.g. der Genosse / die Genossin — comrade)

-NIS-SAL (70%) (NEUTER)
das Bedürfnis (need), das Ereignis (event), das Schicksal (fate)

-AL -AN -AR -ÄR AT
-ENT -ETT -IER -IV
-O -ON (FOREIGN LOAN) (NEUTER)
IF the suffixes -al, -an, -ar, -är, -at, -ent, -ett, -ier, -iv-, -o, -on are used to refer to male persons, they take the masculine: der Student, der Militär (military man), der Kanadier (male Canadian).
Otherwise, these suffixes are generally neuter: das Lineal (ruler), das Organ, das Formular, das Militär (military), das Sekretariat (secretary), das Talent, das Etikett (label, tag), das Papier (paper), das Adjecktiv (adjective), das Büro (office), das Mikrophon










              FEMALE
||||||||||||||||||||||||||||||||||||
||||||||||||||||||||||||||||||||||||


AIRPLANE, MOTORCYCLE, SHIP (makes, models, names) (FEMALE)
Common exceptions: Means of transportation that maintain the gender of the base word, e.g.: der Airbus, der Storch, der “Albatros”, das “Möwchen”
ANIMALS (FEMALE)
die Gans (goose), die Kuh (cow), die Sau (sow), die Henne (hen)

NUMERALS (used as nouns) (FEMALE)
die Eins (1), die Zwei (2), die Sechzehn (16), die Million, die Milliarde (billion) Common exceptions: Quantity expressions such as das Dutzend, das Hundert, das Tausend
RIVERS (Germany, Austria, Switzerland) (FEMALE)
die Donau, die Elbe, die Havel, die Mosel, die Oder, die Weser Some exceptions: der Inn, der Lech, der Main, der Neckar, der Rhein
TREES, FRUITS, FLOWERS (FEMALE)
die Eiche (oak), die Pflaume (plum), die Tulpe (tulip)
Exceptions: der Ahorn (maple), der Apfel (apple), der Löwenzahn (dandelion)


-A -ANZ -ENZ -SCHAFT
-EI -IE -HEIT -KEIT -IK 
-SION -TION -SIS -TÄT
-UNG -UR -SCHAFT (FEMALE)
Common exceptions: das Sofa, das Genie (genius) ,der Atlantik, der Katholik, das Mosaik, der Pazifik, das Abitur (university-entrance diploma), das Purpur (purple)

-IN -FRAU (female of male) (FEMALE)
die Studentin (female student), die Kauffrau (businesswoman)

-E (90%) (FEMALE)
Some common exceptions: der Käse (cheese), das Auge (eye), das Ende (end), das Interesse (interest), most nouns that end with -e but begin with Ge- (e.g. das Genie)

-T (most from verbs) (FEMALE)
Some common exceptions der Dienst (service), der Durst (thirst), das Gift (poison)
